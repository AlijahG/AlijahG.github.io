About

Zoned In Magezine was created for all of those who enjoy looking at a multitude of art in different media styles. Inspired by sites such as Tumblr, Pinterest, and Google. "Zoned In Mag" for short, displays a collection of work that is posted for each day of the week. The work shown is deem as inspirational and cool by Zone In Mag for those of you to "Zone In" and get inspired from. Or simply a site to look at.
